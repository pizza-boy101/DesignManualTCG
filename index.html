  • 2+ players

    • How to play:

    Player 1 will be the Oldest player.


    Player 1 will flip over two random cards, if the cards are not a pair, the turn ends.


    When a player flips over a pair of cards, one point is awarded, the player then rolls the colored dice. Based on the color of the top side of the rolled dice, the player will pick up the subsequently-colored chance card.


    The chance card will contain either a Bonus or a Forfeit. The player can opt out of the forfeit. If the forfeit is complete, the color of the chance card indicates how many points the player receives. The turn ends after the bonus or forfeit is completed/forfeit opted out


    Most points after all cards are faced up and paired wins!
